What makes it different

Profanity-free everywhere
Enforced before any content is stored — not reported after. Applies to posts, comments, DMs, usernames, event titles, ads and news submissions without exception.
Animal welfare above all
Abuse, trafficking and exploitation signals receive the highest priority escalation — overriding engagement, monetization, growth and creator reach.
News must be verified
Tiered by source: institutional publisher → verified professional → community contributor → general submission. Each tier has its own review path before amplification.
Advertising is verified
Vets, pet food sellers, trainers and NGOs can advertise — but only after identity verification, creative review and mandatory disclosure labelling. No ad bypasses review.
Every action is auditable
Moderation decisions, ad approvals, news publishing, call recording consent — all leave an immutable, queryable audit trail. Enforcement is institutional, not arbitrary.
