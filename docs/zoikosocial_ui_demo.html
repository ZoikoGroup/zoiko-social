Heat season reminder: pavement temperatures above 50°C can cause severe paw pad burns in dogs within 60 seconds. The simple five-second test — hold the back of your hand on the pavement for five seconds; if it's too hot for your hand it's too hot for your dog. Walk early morning or after sundown. Carry water. Watch for excessive panting or lifting of paws.
