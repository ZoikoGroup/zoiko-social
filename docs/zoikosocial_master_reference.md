## What Is ZoikoSocial

ZoikoSocial is a **governed hybrid social media platform** built by Zoiko Media Corp, headquartered in Sacramento, CA. It combines the feature surfaces of Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, TikTok and X — but operates under a strict safety-first doctrine built specifically for **animal lovers, climate & environment communities, and science & technology audiences**.

Unlike general-purpose social platforms that prioritize engagement above all, ZoikoSocial treats animal welfare, verified news, and profanity prevention as hard technical requirements — not optional policies. Every piece of content, every advertisement, every message, and every transaction passes through a governed safety pipeline before it reaches any user.

> **Note:** ZoikoSocial is not connected to Meta (Facebook/Instagram/WhatsApp), ByteDance (TikTok), or X Corp. The "Built from" reference means feature inspiration only — ZoikoSocial is a fully independent platform with its own identity, infrastructure, data, and governance.

## Core Doctrine

Five non-negotiable principles that govern every engineering and product decision. These cannot be overridden by revenue, growth, engagement, or advertiser pressure.

### Principle 01 — Profanity-Free Everywhere
Enforced before any content is stored — not reported after. Applies to posts, comments, DMs, usernames, bios, event titles, ads, news submissions and community names. No exceptions, no paid exemptions.

### Principle 02 — Animal Welfare Above All
Abuse, trafficking, neglect and exploitation signals receive the highest priority escalation — overriding engagement, monetization, growth and creator reach. High-risk cases go to specialist human reviewers, not just automated filters.

### Principle 03 — News Must Be Verified
News is tiered by source credibility: institutional publisher → verified professional → community contributor → general submission. Each tier has its own review path before any amplification. Ad spend can never buy a news badge.

### Principle 04 — Advertising Is Verified
Every advertiser must pass identity verification and creative review. All ads carry mandatory disclosure labels. No ad bypasses the review pipeline regardless of budget or account status.

### Principle 05 — Every Action Is Auditable
Moderation decisions, ad approvals, news publishing, breeding approvals, fundraiser payouts, call recording consent — all leave an immutable, queryable audit trail. Enforcement is institutional, not arbitrary.

### Principle 06 — Safety Is Never Paywalled
Premium plans improve the experience. They cannot buy safety exemptions, bypass moderation, skip content review, override age controls, or grant access to suspended accounts. Commercial and trust systems operate independently.

## Verified News

| Tier | Source type | Review process | Badge |
|---|---|---|---|

## Adoption & Rescue

A dedicated surface for posting and finding animals available for adoption. Only verified rescues and shelters can publish listings.

**Flow:**
```
Rescue/Shelter verified → Listing created (animal photo, health status, vaccinations, temperament) →
Safety & welfare scan → Published → User browses (species, age, location, size filters) →
Adoption enquiry sent → Rescue manages application → Outcome recorded → Follow-up welfare check
```

**Listing includes**
- Animal name, species, breed, age
- Photos and short video
- Health and vaccination status
- Temperament and known behaviours
- Rescue location and distance from viewer
- Adoption fee (if any) and conditions

**Safety controls**
- Only verified rescues can post
- No anonymous or unverified listings
- Price flagging for trafficking signals
- Repeat listing detection
- Post-adoption welfare follow-up prompt
- Welfare escalation if signals detected

## Products Marketplace

A verified shop for ethical pet-related goods. Not an animal trading marketplace — products only. Sellers must pass identity and category verification before any listing goes live.

**Allowed categories**
- Pet food and treats (verified nutritional claims)
- Accessories — collars, harnesses, leashes, toys
- Bedding, crates, carriers, tanks, cages
- Grooming products — shampoos, brushes, clippers
- Training aids — clickers, treat pouches
- Health supplements (reviewed claims only)
- Climate / eco-friendly pet products

**Blocked / restricted**
- No live animals (use Adoption module)
- No unverified medications or treatments
- No deceptive health or nutrition claims
- No products promoting unethical breeding
- No wildlife or endangered species products
- No shock/aversive training tools (restricted)

**Flow:**
```
Seller verified → Listing submitted (title, photos, description, price, species tag) →
Category policy applied → Safety/claims scan → Product review if needed →
Published → Order placed → Payment → Fulfilment → Buyer review → Dispute if needed
```

## Pet Care Marketplace

A location-based, two-sided marketplace connecting pet owners who need care with verified service providers. Booking is hourly or by session with escrow payment.

| Role | What they do | What they need to provide |
|---|---|---|
| **Seeker (Pet Owner)** | Searches by location, species, service type, availability, hourly rate. Sends booking request. Confirms completion and leaves a review. | Pet profile, location, preferred dates/times, special instructions |
| **Service Giver (Care Provider)** | Creates a verified profile with service types, species expertise, hourly rate and availability calendar. Accepts/declines booking requests. | Identity verification, background check, species expertise declaration, service area, rates, availability |

**Service types:**

| Service | Pricing model | Species | Special controls |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Dog Walking** | Per hour or per walk | Dogs | Optional live GPS tracking, group walk size limit |
| **Home Sitting** | Per day or per night | All pets | Owner photo updates, emergency contact required |
| **Boarding** | Per night at provider's home | Dogs, cats, small mammals | Home suitability check, capacity limit per booking |
| **Grooming** | Per session | Dogs, cats, birds | Species and coat type listed; equipment safety review |
| **Vet Transport** | Per trip | All pets | Vehicle suitability declaration |
| **Training Sessions** | Per hour or per session pack | Dogs, cats, birds | Verified trainer qualification; method declaration (positive only) |

**Flow:**
```
Service Giver registers & verified → Sets services, rates, calendar, species →
Seeker searches (location + species + service + date) → Sends booking request →
Giver accepts → Payment held in escrow → Service delivered →
Seeker confirms → Payment released to giver → Both parties review each other
```

> Abuse or welfare incident reported during a booking automatically suspends the giver's listing and opens a trust & safety case. Evidence from the booking record is attached automatically.

## Breeding Match

A carefully governed module for connecting owners who want to ethically pair their pets. Health certification is required before any listing goes live. Commercial breeding operations are blocked.

**Flow:**
```
Pet owner creates pet profile (species, breed, DOB, health certs, vaccination status) →
Opts into breeding pool → Listing reviewed (animal welfare + trafficking check) →
Published → Algorithm matches by: breed compatibility, geography, health status, owner preference →
Interest sent to both owners → Both accept → Agreement record created →
Breeding takes place → Litter registered → 6-week follow-up welfare check → Outcome recorded
```

**Pet profile must include**
- Species and verified breed
- Date of birth and weight
- Health certificates (uploaded, reviewed)
- Current vaccination status
- Known genetic conditions
- Owner identity (verified account required)

**Safety gates**
- No anonymous listings — owner must be verified
- Health cert required; no cert = no listing
- Repeat listing detection (mill flagging)
- Commercial breeding operations blocked
- Litter outcome tracking — welfare follow-up
- Escalation if suspicious patterns detected

## Lost & Found

A dedicated, location-aware surface for reporting missing or found animals. Reports trigger community alerts to nearby users and relevant local groups automatically.

**Reporting a lost animal**
- Select report type: Lost
- Upload photo(s) of the animal
- Species, breed, name, colour, microchip number
- Last seen location (map pin or address)
- Date and time last seen
- Distinguishing marks or features
- Owner contact method (phone / in-app)
- Reward offered (optional, cannot be money-gated)

**Reporting a found animal**
- Select report type: Found
- Upload photo(s) of the animal
- Species, breed estimate, colour, any tags/collar
- Location found (map pin)
- Current status: with me / taken to shelter / at location
- Finder contact method
- Shelter or vet drop-off details if applicable

**Flow:**
```
Report submitted → Safety scan (no personal data exposure, no scam patterns) →
Published → Push alert sent to users within configurable radius (e.g. 10km) →
Alert posted in relevant local community groups →
Possible match detection: Found reports compared against active Lost reports →
Match suggested → Reporter notified → Case resolved or expires after 90 days →
Outcome logged (reunited / adopted by finder / taken to shelter / unresolved)
```

> If a Found animal shows signs of abuse, neglect or injury, the report can be escalated directly to the animal welfare review queue from the report form. Rescues and shelters in the area are also notified.

**Data privacy rules:**

| Data stored | Purpose | Privacy rule |
|---|---|---|
| Geo-coordinates of last seen / found location | Map display, proximity alerts, match detection | Precise location shown only to verified users; general area shown publicly |
| Owner contact details | Finder can reach owner | Phone number hidden by default; contact through in-app messaging only unless owner opts to show it |
| Animal photos | Identification and matching | Public; reverse-image search fraud protection applied |
| Microchip number | Official identification cross-reference | Stored but not shown publicly; used for system matching only |
| Report outcome | Platform quality and welfare metrics | Aggregated for analytics; individual outcome private |

## Pet Diary

A personal milestone journal for each pet profile. Private by default, shareable by choice. The diary builds a living memory of a pet's life from arrival to their final days.

| Entry type | What it captures | Shareable? |
|---|---|---|
| **Milestone** | First walk, first swim, first trick, first day home, first birthday | Yes — to feed, story or community |
| **Birthday** | Annual celebration log with photos and well-wishes from followers | Yes — triggers birthday card from community |
| **New Trick** | Short video or photo log: sit, shake, roll over, agility step, new behaviour | Yes — to reels or community |
| **Weight Log** | Periodic weight entry, creates a chart over time, links to vet visits | Private by default |
| **Vet Visit** | Date, clinic, vet name, reason, outcome, next appointment — links to Health Passport | Private by default |
| **Memory / Note** | Free-text entry with optional photo: funny moment, behaviour note, travel memory | Owner's choice |
| **Adoption Day** | The day the pet came home — annual "gotcha day" reminder generated | Yes — shareable as a post |
| **Rainbow Bridge** | Memorial entry when a pet passes; optional public tribute and community condolences | Optional public tribute |

> Pet Diary entries that are shared to the feed or stories pass through the standard safety pipeline. Rainbow Bridge tributes receive a compassionate moderation queue — no automated rejection for emotional content.

**Features:**

| Feature | How it works |
|---|---|
| **Timeline view** | All entries shown chronologically as a scrollable life timeline per pet |
| **Weight chart** | Auto-generated graph from weight log entries; shareable with vet |
| **Annual summary** | End-of-year auto-generated "Year in review" for each pet with highlights from diary entries |
| **Reminder system** | Birthday reminder, vaccination due (from Health Passport), vet appointment, annual gotcha day |
| **Privacy control** | Per-entry: Private / Friends only / Public. Diary itself defaults to private unless owner changes |
| **Export** | Download full diary as PDF (for vet records, insurance, keepsake printing) |

## Health Passport

A digital, shareable health record for each pet. Stores vaccinations, medications, vet visits, deworming and weight history in one place. Linked to the Pet Diary and Breeding Match modules.

| Record type | Fields captured | Reminder generated? |
|---|---|---|
| **Vaccination** | Vaccine name, date given, batch number, vet, next due date, clinic | Yes — 4 weeks before next due |
| **Deworming / Flea treatment** | Product name, date given, next due interval | Yes — on schedule |
| **Vet visit** | Date, clinic, vet name, reason, diagnosis, treatment, follow-up notes | Yes if follow-up scheduled |
| **Medication** | Drug name, dosage, frequency, start/end date, prescribing vet | Yes — daily dose reminder (optional) |
| **Weight history** | Date and weight; chart view; shared with vet on request | No (manual entry) |
| **Allergies** | Known allergens, severity, date confirmed | Surfaces as warning in Product search |
| **Microchip** | Chip number, registry, implant date, vet who implanted | No; used for Lost & Found matching |
| **Insurance** | Provider, policy number, expiry, coverage notes | Yes — 60 days before renewal |

> Health Passport data is private by default. It can be shared with a vet via a time-limited, read-only link. It is never used for advertising targeting. It links to the Breeding Match module to verify health certification requirements.

## Animal Taxonomy

Which animals are supported, at what level, and with what restrictions. The tier determines which modules are available for each animal type.

### Tier 1 — Full Access
All modules: social, communities, care, diary, health passport, products, adoption, events, breeding match (where legal).

Dogs (all breeds) · Cats (all breeds) · Rabbits · Guinea Pigs · Hamsters & Gerbils · Chinchillas · Parrots · Budgies / Parakeets · Cockatiels · Lovebirds · Canaries & Finches · Freshwater Fish · Marine / Reef Fish · Turtles & Tortoises · Bearded Dragons · Leopard Geckos · Horses & Ponies · Donkeys & Mules

### Tier 2 — Limited (No Breeding Match; Care Only)
Social, communities, diary, health passport, adoption, vet finder — but no breeding match module. Subject to jurisdiction compliance.

Ferrets · Hedgehogs · Sugar Gliders · Degus · Ball Pythons · Corn Snakes · Iguanas · Blue-Tongue Skinks · Axolotls · Tarantulas · Ducks (domestic) · Chickens (backyard) · Goats (companion) · Miniature Pigs · Alpacas & Llamas

### Tier 3 — Rescue & Welfare Only
No commerce, no breeding, no products. Rescue organizations and licensed rehabilitators only. Reports and welfare alerts permitted.

Owls & Raptors · Foxes (rescued) · Deer & Fawns · Hedgehogs (wild) · Badgers · Bats · Seabirds · Marine Mammals (monitoring only) · Primates (rescue only, no trade)

### Blocked — Not Permitted on Platform
No listings, no communities, no breeding, no commerce. Any content involving these is escalated to welfare review and potentially law enforcement.

CITES Appendix I species · Big cats (lions, tigers, leopards) · Great apes · Venomous/dangerous without permit · Shark fins / wildlife parts · Any trafficked or illegally sourced animal
